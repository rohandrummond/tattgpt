Provide me with details like style, placement, size and colour and I will generate some concepts for you to consider. If you like an idea, you can also generate an image for it and add it to your personal list of saved ideas. I'm built with a combination of Angular, .NET, PostgreSQL (via Supabase), GPT and DALL-E.
